Tracklist ✅

01 Day One Ritual 2 min · Start clean, start simple

Overview: Hydrate, breathe, decide. You start the day with a tiny win that tells your brain “I keep promises to myself.”

  1. Pour a full glass of water. Drink it.
  2. Take 3 slow breaths: in 4s, hold 2s, out 6s.
  3. Write one line in your notebook: Today I choose sober.
  4. Optional: mirror selfie (private) to mark the decision.

Why it works: micro-wins + hydration reduce friction and give a quick dopamine bump without chaos.

Upgrade: add a 30-second posture reset (stand tall, shoulders down, chin level).

02 Move (5-Minute Flow) 5 min · Energy > excuses

Overview: One song. Whole body on. Movement beats rumination and primes focus.

  1. Warm: 60s march in place.
  2. Strength: 30s squats → 30s wall push-ups.
  3. Repeat the pair once.
  4. Stretch: 60s hamstrings + shoulders.
  5. Breath: 10 slow exhales, count them out.
  • Low-impact: chair sit-stands + countertop push-ups.
  • Spicy: add 30s plank between sets.

Upgrade: pick a 5-min hype track; when the song ends, you’re done.

03 Mindset (One-Line) 1 min · Rewire self-talk

Overview: A single line shapes attention and action. Keep it punchy and personal.

  1. Pick or write your line. Examples below.
  2. Say it out loud, twice, looking at yourself.
  3. Set it on your phone lock screen or sticky note.

Ideas:

  • I don’t need a drink to be iconic.
  • Clarity over chaos.
  • Cravings are waves — I surf them.
  • I am the main character of my life.

Upgrade: record a 10s voice note of your line and play it before you leave the house.

04 Night Reset 3 min · Protect tomorrow

Overview: Set the stage for an easy morning. Tomorrow’s win starts tonight.

  1. Lay out: water, notebook, morning outfit.
  2. Do a 15-item tidy sprint (set a 2-min timer).
  3. Write one win from today. Lights down, screens away.

If you miss it: Do a 60-second “micro-reset”: set out notebook + water, one deep breath, done.

Upgrade: phone sleeps outside the bedroom; alarm across the room.

Bonus Community Check-In 1–2 min · Accountability, zero judgment

Overview: Tiny daily touchpoints keep momentum without pressure.

  • Daily: send one emoji or 3 words: 🟢 focused, hydrated, calm
  • Weekly: 60-sec voice note: win / wobble / plan.
  • Reset protocol: if you slip, text “Resetting now” + do the Day One Ritual.

Upgrade: schedule a 10-min Sunday sync to set one goal for the week.
