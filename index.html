Tracklist

Track 01 — Day One Ritual

Two minutes. One promise. Start clean.

  • Drink a full glass of water.
  • 3 slow breaths (4 in, 2 hold, 6 out).
  • Write: Today I choose sober.
  • Optional: mirror selfie for yourself.

Track 02 — Move (5-Minute Flow)

Five minutes. Energy > excuses.

  • 60s march in place.
  • 30s squats → 30s wall push-ups.
  • Repeat once.
  • 60s stretch (hamstrings + shoulders).
  • 10 slow breaths to close.

Track 03 — Mindset (One-Line)

One line. Total focus.

  • Write a powerful line like “Clarity over chaos.”
  • Say it twice while looking in the mirror.
  • Set it as phone lock screen or sticky note.

Track 04 — Night Reset

Three minutes. Protect tomorrow.

  • Set out water, notebook, morning outfit.
  • 15-item tidy sprint (timer on).
  • Write one win from today → lights out.

Bonus Track — Community Check-In

Quick daily accountability.

  • Send an emoji or 3 words to your buddy.
  • Weekly: 60-sec voice note about wins & wobbles.
  • Missed a day? Reset and keep moving forward.
